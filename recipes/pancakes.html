Good Old Fashioned Pancakes

Description

Grandma's favorite pancake recipe. These are, hands down, the most incredible pancakes you will ever have. Pancakes this good should be illegal.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon alt, or more to taste
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

Steps

  1. In large bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, salt and sugar
  2. Make a well in the center and pour milk, egg, and melted butter; mix until smooth
  3. Heat lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat
  4. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto griddle for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot
